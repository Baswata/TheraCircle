About TheraCircle

TheraCircle is a community-driven platform designed to connect individuals and families with licensed occupational therapists and a supportive network. Our mission is to provide a safe space for families and individuals seeking therapy resources, daily encouragement, and a compassionate community.

Whether you're a parent looking for advice, someone seeking mental health support, or a therapist aiming to help, TheraCircle is here to connect and support you every step of the way.
